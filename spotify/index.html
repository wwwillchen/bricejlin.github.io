As an avid music listener and legal music advocate, I’ve always longed for a platform where legally streaming music could be organized into personal playlists. Spotify is that platform for me. What I especially love about Spotify is its aspiration to be more convenient than piracy. I think Spotify tackles this problem in a very interesting way that encourages music streams through social interaction and playlist sharing.

My interest in Spotify was especially enforced upon visiting the Spotify office to chat with a developer that gave a coding session at Startup Institute. In our conversation, we spoke a little about the way the teams collaborate in squads, tribes and chapters and I thought that was a really interesting process that encourages open communication.

Since walking away from the conversation, I started to think more about the company's challenges as it continues to expand and came up with some ideas that I could possibly contribute to as an intern:

Discover: Spotify has huge amounts of data to leverage - user behavior, artist trends, playlists, etc. I would love to be with a team working on machine learning and finding ways to help Spotify make more personalized recommendations for Spotify users.

Follow: In addition to the community forums, I think Spotify has the potential to further engage and create strong community interaction within the app experience. Instead of only suggesting Facebook friends and famous artists, I’d love to explore the possibility of utilizing algorithms that suggest "Who to Follow" based on a match between their musical tastes and mine.

Social: People connect over the internet via shared interests. We have accounts online that share our wish lists, book lists, photo lists, etc. I think one way Spotify can differentiate itself from other music subscriber services is by strengthening its social connections between users. By allowing Spotify users to build a more customized app profile, it'll give them more things to talk about. One feature can be a personal timeline of songs that a person connected with while they were at a certain age - (Spotiline? Songline? Haha, I don’t know). Then from a technical standpoint, on the Follow page, we can try to connect people with similar interests/profiles.
